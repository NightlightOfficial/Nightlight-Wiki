# Creator Hub
Place for Marketplace creators to see how their items are doing

### What is it?

Creator Hub allows you to publish your items and see analytics and statistics of them!

### Submitting items

You can submit new items by clicking the "Hell yeah" button on the Creator Hub page, or by the "Submit new item" if you submitted items before.

### What can I see about my items?

* Total amount of sold items
* Total amount of items published
* Total amount of currently equipped items by other users

#### Individual items

* How many times was the item sold
* How many users have it currently equipped
* How many NCPs you have earned from selling it

:::note

Creator Hub is currently in Beta and will offer many more features in the future!

:::
