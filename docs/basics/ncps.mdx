# NCPs
NCPs or Nightlight Community Points are a virtual currency on Nightlight used to be exchanged for items in the Nightlight Marketplace

### What that, what that do tho..

Absolutely wonderful question, user! NCPs can be exchanged in the Nightlight Marketplace for items! They currently don't have any other use case.

### Okay but how do I get em⁉️

Another banger question, user! There are actually many ways to obtain NCPs on Nightlight!

| Name                                 | Amount of NCP received |
| ------------------------------------ | ---------------------- |
| Nighty's Humble Reward (Daily Login) | 10 or 15               |
| Receiving 10 or 20 likes on a post   | 10 or 20               |
| Obtaining a badge                    | 20                     |
| Referring a friend                   | 50                     |
| Selling an item on the Marketplace   | ½ of the item's price  |
| Stellnix (Minecraft server)          | Dependent              |
