# Leaderboard
Fight for the podium on the Nightlight Leaderboard!

### What is it?

Nightlight Leaderboard allows users to compete for the podium in many different categories! It's more of a silly feature than anything, really :3

### What categories can I compete in?

There are currently 7 different categories to compete in:

* Level
    * Users with the highest levels
* NCP
    * Users with the biggest amount of NCPs currently available
* Time Spent
    * Users who have spent the most time **actively** scrolling through Nightlight
* Streaks
    * Users with the highest streaks
* Followers
    * Users with the current most amount of followers
* Likes
    * Users with the most amount of likes across all of their posts
* Items Sold
    * Users with the most amount of items sold
