# Items
Express yourself on Nightlight with different and unique items from other users!

### What are items?

Items on Nightlight are collectible items which allow users like you to express themselves and make their posts and profiles more unique and personal to them.

### How can I get items?

You can get items on the [Nightlight Marketplace](https://wiki.night-light.cz/basics/items-and-creators/marketplace).

### How do I equip/unequip my items?

You can equip your items through your Inventory. If you want to unequip currently selected item, you can click on it again or equip another item.

### Item Types

There are currently 7 different item types, each allowing you to express yourself differently.

| Type                | Description                                                                                                         | Price range (NCP)     |
| ------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------- |
| Profile Decorations | Allows you to decorate your profile picture! These decorations are either static or animated.                       | 30 - 100              |
| Profile Animations  | Have a cool animation run across your profile when someone visits it!                                               | 120                   |
| Profile Music       | Plays music upon visiting your profile.                                                                             | 50 - 100              |
| Voicepacks          | Narrate your Nightlight experience! Plays different sounds or voice lines when you do certain things on Nightlight! | 50 - 150              |
| Titles              | Have a title next to your display name on posts and profile!                                                        | 20 - 50               |
| Backplates          | Makes your title even shinier with a stylish background!                                                            | 30 - 60               |
| Themes              | Turn Nightlight upside down with colorful themes! Change the Nightlight look to your preferences.                   | Currently unavailable |
